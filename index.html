About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, under the advisory of Professor Yousef Saad. My research interests lie primarily in numerical linear algebra, high-performance computing, and data science.

Education

I received my M.S. (2019) in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, U.S. I received my M.S. (2016) in Computational Mathematics from the Beihang University, China. My advisor was Professor Vladimir Shaydurov. I received my B.S. (2013) in Computational Mathematics from the Beihang University, China.
